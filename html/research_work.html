On-going Project

Guwahati, Silchar, Nalbari, Nagaon, Sivsagar in Assam as ongoing project locations have been covered under the National Clean Air Programme Project. This research aims to assess the contribution of emissions from multiple sources due to different activities. Consequently, ambient air quality monitoring and analyses from various monitoring stations have been conducted. This research has covered the concentration of numerous pollutants, including PM10, PM2.5, SOx, NOx, etc. Additionally, meteorological conditions in different regions have been monitored simultaneously.
