About Me

I am PhD research scholar in Civil Engineering at Indian Institute of Technology Guwahati and Indian Institute of Technology (BHU) Varanasi. After completion master's in Civil Engineering (Environmental Engineering) from Maharishi Markandeshwar University, Mullana, Ambala, in 2021, I have joined as research scholar. Evaluation of sewage treatment plant to achieve zero liquid discharge which is running on activated sludge process to initiate green (swachh) campus was my project work in master degree. This work has been presented in International Conference on Recent Advances in Applied Sciences (ICRAAS-2022), entitled "Design and performace analysis of ASP based sewage treatment plant of university for ZLD". I completed my bachelor's degree from Techno India University, West Bengal, in 2018. Diploma in Building Design course certified from CADD Center, Kolkata during bachelor degree. Furthermore, I am an Academic content writer and having more than one year of experience with completion over 80 writings including projects, theses, and research articles for abroad and Indian institutes. I am a freelaning building designer, having more than four years of experience with profeciency of Autodesk AutoCAD, Staad-Pro, ETABS, Revit Architecture, Sketchup, AutoCAD Civil 3D, Primavera, MS Excel, MS Project, Autodesk 3ds Max, ArcGIS, Python, QGIS, Origin Pro, WAVE, SUMO model, Traffic model. Now I am working with traffic and air quality modeling tools to improve urban air quality with traffic management as my PhD work. Rather than the environmental and transportation field, I also have an interest in wastewater treatment and solid waste management. I have also working on Machine learning and data science to integrate with my study to improve accuracy of my work. At the same time, I am working as researcher for National Clean Air Programme conducted by MoEF&CC to analyse air quality at different regions.
